Promises

  • Pending - when a promise is created, it calls an asynchronous operation
  • Unsettled - While the operation is taking place
  • Settled - Once the operation has completed. Can result in two different outcomes:
  • Resolved - the asynchronous operation was successfully completed
  • Rejected - the asynchronous operation didn't work as expected, it wasn't successfully completed or resulted in an error
